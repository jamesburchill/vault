# About

The Vault is the public field-notes layer behind my work on governing intelligent systems.

It is intentionally simple: durable content, stable URLs, plain Markdown, and enough structure for people, search engines, and AI systems to understand what is here without depending on a heavy publishing platform.

The current centre of gravity is drift, operational coherence, control surfaces, provenance, AI governance, and the practical systems work required to keep intelligent software aligned with real-world intent.

## How It Is Organized

- Dated writing lives under `content/YYYY/MM/`.
- Images and supporting files live under `assets/`.
- Stable pages such as About and Now live at the top level.
- The content is published as a static website with archive pages, feeds, and machine-readable discovery files.

## Why This Exists

The web has become increasingly noisy, dynamic, and difficult to inspect. This vault is a deliberate move in the other direction.

The public website is the readable version. The underlying Markdown is the durable source.
