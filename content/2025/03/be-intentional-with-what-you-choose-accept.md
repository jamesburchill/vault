# Be Intentional with What You Choose & Accept

![Be Intentional with What You Choose & Accept](/assets/2025/03/be-intentional-with-what-you-choose-accept-cold-snow-wood-66100-scaled.jpeg)


## **For Your Life**

*   Surround yourself with **positive** people, situations, thoughts, and actions. _(This naturally filters out negativity.)_
*   Avoid contributing to negativity—sometimes, **silence is the best response**.

## **For Your Body**

*   Fuel yourself wisely: **more water, fewer processed foods**. _(Minimize substances that cloud your perception, like excessive alcohol.)_
*   Take care of your body:
    *   Engage in **resistance training** and get outside for a walk.
    *   Prioritize **rest and quality sleep**—your body repairs itself when you do.

## **For Your Mind, Spirit & Heart**

*   **Nourish your mind**: read, meditate, journal, and listen to meaningful music. _(Avoid mental junk food—populism, propaganda, and fear-based news.)_
*   **Commit fully** to one path at a time—until it no longer serves you. _(Chasing the next big thing, pseudo-experts, or fads only scatters your focus.)_
*   **Trust wisely**—people are inherently good but deserve your discernment.
*   **Practice gratitude**—you already have more than most will ever have.

## **When Faced with a Choice**

*   Choose **simplicity over complexity**.
*   Choose **quiet over noise**.
*   Choose **listening over speaking**.

And if you’re uncertain …

> _**The answer is NO**._
