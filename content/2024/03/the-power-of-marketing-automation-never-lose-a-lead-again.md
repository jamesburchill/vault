# The Power of Marketing Automation: Never Lose a Lead Again

![The Power of Marketing Automation: Never Lose a Lead Again](/assets/2024/03/the-power-of-marketing-automation-never-lose-a-lead-again-red-clock-bw-copy.jpg)


I’ve said it before and I’ll say it again: **“People buy when THEY’RE ready to buy … and NOT BEFORE!”** Nearly **7 out of 10 people** who say “No thanks” today will say **“Yes!” within 12 months.** Let that sink in for a moment… that means you need to keep asking—or, put in a more palatable way—**keep following up.**

But who has time? It’s tedious, boring work. Remembering to send that email, mail that card, or call that client? **Ick.** And let’s not forget the rejection, the voicemail tag—it’s enough to make you want to run away and join the circus. But what if there was a better way? What if a hundred digital hands lifted you up and did all the ‘dirty work’ so you didn’t have to?

Good news: **this ‘nirvana’ exists** and has for some time. Welcome to the wonderful world of **marketing automation.** Unlike a fairy tale, this story is real—no monsters, no damsels in distress, and no toads to kiss. Just a straightforward approach: **define the process you use to court a client (marketing or selling to them) and then automate it.**

Today, marketing automation is nothing new—it’s actually **‘old hat’** in terms of solutions. What makes it exciting are the **technological advances** made over the past 20 years. Modern systems can now **adapt and change messaging flows**based on a prospect’s interactions—bordering on **adaptive intelligence.**

### Bottom Line: Stay in the Game

If you want to increase your sales, explore **marketing automation platforms** that fit your needs. Whether you need a sophisticated, dynamic system or something simpler like **sequential email follow-ups**, there’s a solution out there for you.

Remember, **70% of people who said no today will say yes within a year.** Will you still be following up with them?

**TIP:** If you’re using marketing automation, YOU WILL BE 😈 #Winner
