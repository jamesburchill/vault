# Meet AUALA, my new 2024 Honda GoldWing (First ride impressions)

![Meet AUALA, my new 2024 Honda GoldWing (First ride impressions)](/assets/2024/04/meet-auala-my-new-2024-honda-goldwing-first-ride-impressions-maxresdefault.jpg)


Today (April 6, 2024) I picked up my new Honda GoldWing DCT. I’ve named her AUALA, Latin for Gold Wing, today was my very first time riding my new bike. Also my first time riding a GoldWing too. Talk about underselling the experience! This bike blows me away, and I’m so excited to get out riding again tomorrow if the weather cooperates. I’ll get a camera rigged up for the run if I can. Anyway, if you’re a wing rider you know what I’m talking about.
